IWR Fund Description

IWR tracks a market-cap-weighted index of the 800-smallest companies in the Russell 1000.

IWR Factset Analytics Insight

IWR delivers the US mid-cap market well via a large and representative basket of stocks. The fund ventures a little deeper into the large-cap space, so its top 10 holdings differ from those of our index. This large-cap bias shows up in slightly lower market risk to our midcap benchmark. The size tilt also plays out in subtle sector biases. IWR is one of the best choices for retail investors and frequent traders alike. It's easy to deal in IWR at any scale. The fund tracks well and even earns some of its fees back via securities lending.

IWR MSCI ESG Analytics Insight

iShares Russell Mid-Cap ETF has an MSCI ESG Fund Rating of BBB based on a score of 5.29 out of 10. The MSCI ESG Fund Rating measures the resiliency of portfolios to long-term risks and opportunities arising from environmental, social, and governance factors. ESG Fund Ratings range from best (AAA) to worst (CCC). Highly rated funds consist of companies that tend to show strong and/or improving management of financially relevant environmental, social and governance issues. These companies may be more resilient to disruptions arising from ESG events.

The funds Peer Rank reflects the ranking of a funds MSCI ESG Fund Quality Score against the scores of other funds within the same peer group, as defined by the Thomson Reuters Lipper Global Classification. iShares Russell Mid-Cap ETF ranks in the 36th percentile within its peer group and in the 73th percentile within the global universe of all funds covered by MSCI ESG Fund Ratings.
